Show solidarity to those displaced by the war in Ukraine.

Pryvit means "Hello" in Ukrainian.

This website was created to welcome individuals who were forced to leave Ukraine, and are now living in Ireland. We hope to gather information about company pledges in just one place in order to facilitate access.

If your company is donating items or offering discounts, please complete the form below. It will make it much easier for those who need your help to find you.

I submitted my pledge, what happens next?

You can find on our list of company pledges by clicking on 'FIND OFFER' at the top of the website. If an individual is interested in your offer, they will contact you directly. Please note we are not responsible for any communications between parties.

Why is this important?

So many companies are making pledges to support displaced Ukrainians, but the reality is that when they arrive in Ireland they don't know where to find these. This website was created to become the largest database in Ireland for non-financial corporate supports to Ukrainian refugees.
